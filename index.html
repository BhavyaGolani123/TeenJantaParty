What We Stand For

Our Manifesto

Three pillars that define our commitment to you — as students, as peers, and as the next generation of leaders.

🗣️

Students' Voice

Every student has a perspective worth sharing. TJP will build clear, open channels — suggestion boxes, open councils, and regular student assemblies — so no voice goes unheard. Your ideas shape our school, not just ours.

🏆

Leadership for All

Leadership isn't reserved for a chosen few. We'll create mentorship programmes, inter-house debates, and skill-building workshops so every student — from every house and every year — has the chance to lead, inspire, and grow.

💡

Brightening Campaign

From cleaner classrooms to better lighting on sports fields, from celebrating academic wins to amplifying cultural talent — our Brightening Campaign is about making every space, every event, and every moment in this school radiate excellence.

Who We Are

About TJP

The Teen Janta Party was founded on a single truth: students, when given the platform, can transform a school into something extraordinary.

We aren't just campaigning for positions — we're campaigning for a culture. A culture where academics and arts, sports and sciences, houses and halls all receive equal celebration and equal resources.

With five candidates spanning house captaincy, cultural leadership, sports, and junior representation, TJP offers the most complete, most inclusive team in this election. Every corner of school life is covered.
